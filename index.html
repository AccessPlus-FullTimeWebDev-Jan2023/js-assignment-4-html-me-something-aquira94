My Journey To Becoming a Web Developer

College Years

Future Goals

Now, I am excited to continue my journey as a web developer with LaunchCode. I've already learned a lot during my short time here and I look forward to completing this bootcamp! I want to specialize in front-end development and learn more about emerging technologies like AI.
